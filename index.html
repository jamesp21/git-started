Aang!

Aang is a 12 year old boy who was chosen to be the next Avatar. An Avatar basically acts as an arbiter amoung various people. Starting off as a natural born master of airbending, he must then aquire the skills to also master the other three elements which are: fire, water, and earth. He is covered in arrow tattoos which he got from being a master of airbending. His goal is to stop the fire nation from basically destroying the world.

Aang is a character from the TV series: Avatar, The last Airbender
